Proxy Company Listings & Descriptions

Rammerhead:

Created by binary-person on Github. A fully configurable proxy that works on many sites. The proxy allows users to create a “Session." When they access their session, their LocalStorage and cookies will be synced with Rammerhead. This allows for accurately mocking cookied requests and conveniently saving their logins even if they switch devices. This also enables users to configure a custom HTTP proxy server for Rammerhead to connect to the session.

Light:

Created by networknt on Github. A fast and light with an embedded gateway to wrap third party API's and bring them to the ecosystem of Light-4j frameworks. Community Features:

  • Google Group
  • Gitter Group
  • Subreddit
  • Documentation & Official Website
  • Contribution Guide
  • Official Youtube Channel
  • Stack Overflow

The Light proxy itself includes:

  • High throughput, low latency and small footprint
  • Integrate light-oauth2 to protect un-secured services
  • Built-in load balancer
  • Can be started with Docker or standalone
  • Support HTTP 2.0 protocol on both In and Out connections
  • TLS termination
  • Support REST, GraphQL, and RPC style of API's
  • Centralized logging with ELK, traceabilityId and CorrelationId
  • Collect client and service metrics into InfluxDB and view the dashboard on Grafana
  • Service registry and discovery with Consul or Zookeeper
  • Manage configuration with Light config-server

Doge Unblocker:

Created by DogeNetwork on Github. Doge Unblocker is a site unblocker powered by Ultraviolet. Our service allows you to get around internet restrictions to visit your favorite sites and apps, without worrying about your browsing history being saved or your internet being blocked.

Doge Unblocker Features:

  • Advanced About:Blank Cloaking
  • Hides site from Browser History
  • Clickoff Cloaking
  • Automatic URL Coding
