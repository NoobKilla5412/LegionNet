About The Developers

LegionNet is a web based service used to catalog, archive and share various websites and tools to use for personal entertainment and the counteraction of internet censorship. If you're ever bored in class, your searches are being blocked by Securly or another blocking service, or there's something stopping you from accessing what you want to access, we've got you covered. Our team is based out of Crescent Valley High School and Kings Valley Charter School, Corvallis Oregon.
